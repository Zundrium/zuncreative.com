**OomAgent** builds AI agents that work for you, helping organizations do more with fewer people. The current portal demonstrates how that idea becomes a focused product experience, where the agent is presented as something practical, direct, and ready to use.

The experience is designed around smooth interaction rather than a simple chat interface. In its current form, the emphasis is on a strong desktop flow that supports demonstration, real usage, and further product iteration.

On the technical side, OomAgent uses **LiveKit** as the foundation for real-time media and LLM connectivity, within an **agentic framework** built to support more contextual and task-oriented behavior. Full real-time voice conversations are still being developed, but the architecture is already intentionally aligned with that direction.
