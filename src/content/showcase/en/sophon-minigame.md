The 'Sophon Minigame' is an application that introduces the user to the 'Sophon universe' through a physics-based game concept. The core game mechanic involves aiming a cannon, adjusting its power, and launching projectiles to hit targets.

The game, named 'Sophon Cannon', is designed for short play sessions and can serve as an accessible introduction to the broader context of the Sophon platform. Users can compare their scores with others. Furthermore, it is suggested that the player's performance may have an impact within the Sophon universe, adding an extra dimension to the individual gameplay.
