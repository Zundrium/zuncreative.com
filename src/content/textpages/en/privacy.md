## 1. Interpretation and Definitions

In this privacy policy, the following terms have a specific meaning:

*   **Account:** Your personal access to our services.
*   **Company:** Zun Creative, located at Vlierweg 12, 1032LG Amsterdam.
*   **Cookies:** Small files placed on your device while using our services.
*   **Services:** All activities we offer.
*   **Personal Data:** Any data relating to an identified or identifiable person.
*   **Usage Data:** Automatically collected data about the use of our services.

## 2. Collection and Use of Personal Data

When using our services, we may collect the following personal data:

*   Contact details (such as email address, phone number, address)
*   Identification data (such as name)
*   Usage data (such as IP address, browser data, visit statistics)

Usage data is automatically collected during your use of our services. This may include your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and times of visit. When used via mobile devices, we may collect specific device data.

## 3. Social Media Integration

If you register via social media, we may access certain data from your profile, such as your name and email address. You can decide what additional data you want to share with us.

## 4. Use of Cookies

We use cookies for various purposes:

*   **Necessary cookies:** Essential for the functioning of our services.
*   **Functional cookies:** Remember your preferences for a better user experience.
*   **Analytical cookies:** Help us understand and improve the use of our services.

## 5. Purposes of Data Processing

We process your personal data for:

*   Providing and improving our services
*   Managing your account
*   Communicating about updates and services
*   Complying with legal obligations
*   Analyzing and improving our service delivery

## 6. Sharing of Personal Data

We only share your data when necessary:

*   With service providers who support us
*   In a business acquisition or merger
*   If legally required
*   With your explicit consent

## 7. Security

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against loss or unlawful processing. Although we strive for optimal security, no system can guarantee 100% safety.

## 8. Your Rights

Under the GDPR, you have several rights:

*   Right to access your personal data
*   Right to correct inaccurate data
*   Right to delete your data
*   Right to restrict processing
*   Right to data portability
*   Right to object to processing

## 9. Children

Our services are not intended for persons under 16 years of age. If we discover that we have unintentionally collected data from a minor, we will delete it.

## 10. Changes

We may periodically adjust this privacy policy. In case of significant changes, we will inform you via email or a notification on our website. The most recent version is always available on our website.
