<p>Thanks to COVID social events were cancelled. The client had to find an online alternative for making an event that shows artpieces from over 400 museums in The Netherlands.</p>

      <p>This was done with a three dimensional representation of the Museumplein in Amsterdam, where the event would normally be held. </p>
